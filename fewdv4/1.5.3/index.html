Introduction

I have donned many hats over the years, but none have been more rewarding than that of a software engineer. Each day provides a new opportunity to solve a complex problem, improve upon a process, and create new, innovative tools. It is a unique feeling to see a product that I contributed to being used "in the wild."

I currently work for a major telecommunications company as a Senior Software Engineer. The primary application that I work on is built with AngularJS and Codeigniter.

My driving force is my family, I have a loving, understanding, and beautiful wife and two amazingly wonderful daughters that remind me every day of the reason that I go to work. I believe that it is important to maintain a health work/life balance, and strive to do so.

Where I'm From

I'm born and raised in Central New York. No no, not New York City! Go north about 4 hours north and you'll hit Syracuse. I'm just a hair north on the left side of Oneida lake.

I earned my Bachelors degree from Colorado Technical University in Computer Science, concentrating on web development.
